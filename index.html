After-sales Service售后服务

Professional Repair & Maintenance 专业维修与维护保养

We provide end-to-end after-sales services for commercial/industrial energy storage systems and electric vehicles, covering everything from module-level diagnostics to full system overhaul. 我们为工商业储能系统及电动车辆提供全链条售后服务，涵盖模组级诊断到整系统大修。

Cell / Module / Pack Balancing 电芯模组 Pack 均衡

Precise capacity balancing to restore system performance and extend battery life. 精准容量均衡，恢复系统性能，延长电池寿命。

Communication Fault Repair 通讯故障维修

Diagnosis and resolution of BMS, CAN bus and protocol communication failures. BMS、CAN总线及协议通讯故障诊断与修复。

Thermal Management & Water Cooling 热管理系统 / 水冷机组

Service and repair of liquid cooling systems to maintain optimal operating temperatures. 液冷系统维修保养，确保设备在最佳温度范围内运行。

Firmware & Program Upgrades 程序升级

BMS / EMS software updates to the latest versions for improved safety and efficiency. BMS/EMS软件升级至最新版本，提升安全性与效率。

EV Three-Electric System 乘用车 / 商用车三电故障

Battery, motor and e-control fault diagnosis for passenger and commercial EVs. 乘用车及商用车电池、电机、电控三电系统故障诊断。

Insulation & Safety Inspection 绝缘检测与安全检查

High-voltage insulation resistance testing and safety compliance inspections. 高压绝缘电阻测试及安全合规检查。

About Us关于我们

Who We Are 公司介绍

BoostEnergy AS is a technology innovation company headquartered in Asker, Norway, founded in 2025. We serve Norway, Sweden, Denmark and Finland — the heart of the Nordic region — delivering high-quality digital, engineering and integrated solutions tailored to the needs of the clean-energy transition. BoostEnergy AS 是一家总部位于挪威阿斯克尔的技术创新公司，成立于2025年，服务于挪威、瑞典、丹麦和芬兰——北欧核心地区——为清洁能源转型提供高质量的数字化、工程及综合解决方案。

We combine software and hardware development, technical consulting and hands-on project execution to enable digital transformation and sustainable development across new energy, industry and infrastructure sectors. 我们融合软硬件开发、技术咨询与实地项目执行，推动新能源、工业和基础设施领域的数字化转型与可持续发展。

Our multidisciplinary team brings together expertise from energy engineering, software development, project management and logistics — with extensive experience in both European and international projects. 我们的多元化团队汇聚了能源工程、软件开发、项目管理和物流等领域的专家，拥有丰富的欧洲及国际项目经验。

Mission: To drive industrial digitalization and the green energy transition in the Nordics — and to become the region's leading integrated engineering and technology service provider. 使命：推动北欧工业数字化与绿色能源转型，成为北欧领先的综合工程与技术服务提供商。
